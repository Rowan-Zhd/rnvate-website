BPO Platform Transformation

Led digital and operational transformation across BPO and facilities service platforms for enterprise clients in retail and real estate across the USA, GCC, and Europe.

At a glance
ClientEnterprise BPO Provider
IndustryBPO & Facilities
RegionNew York · USA, GCC, Europe
Timeline2018 – 2019
The challenge

An enterprise BPO and facilities provider served retail and real estate clients across the USA, GCC, and Europe, with distributed teams, inconsistent workflows, and uneven service quality driving avoidable client escalations.

Our approach

How we delivered

RNVATE led digital and operational transformation across the service platforms, coordinating distributed teams across three continents and standardizing how work was done.

We delivered usability improvements that accelerated service resolution and standardized workflows that lifted consistency, bringing client escalations down by around 20% and improving overall client satisfaction.
